About Us

About Tropical Treats

Tropical Treats started as a business idea for college. We wanted to create something that would give students natural energy without all the artificial stuff in regular snacks. Our coconut drops with pineapple and ginger are perfect for studying or just chilling with friends.

Why We Do This

As students ourselves, we know what it's like to need that energy boost during long study sessions. Instead of reaching for processed snacks, we thought - why not make something with real ingredients that actually tastes amazing and gives you natural energy?

Our Product

  • Real coconut, pineapple, and ginger
  • No artificial preservatives
  • Three sizes to fit any budget
  • Perfect for students, study groups, or just treating yourself
