Business Overview

Tropical Treats is a student-founded business specializing in all-natural Pine & Ginger Coconut Drops. Created by Nicole Hart, a 21-year-old college student, our product addresses the growing demand for healthy, natural energy alternatives among students and young adults.

Key Differentiators

  • Authentic Founder Story: Created by a student, for students
  • Natural Ingredients: Real coconut, pineapple, and ginger
  • Targeted Market: Focused on student demographic
  • Strong Brand Identity: Caribbean roots with modern appeal

Market Opportunity

The healthy snack market is experiencing rapid growth, with students increasingly seeking natural alternatives to artificial energy drinks and processed snacks. Tropical Treats is positioned to capture this market through authentic branding and targeted marketing strategies.

Free campus tastings, social media promotion, classroom marketing, and brand awareness campaigns.

Growth Stage

Marketing Strategy

Our comprehensive 4P marketing strategy is designed to maximize market penetration and brand loyalty among our target demographic.

Product Strategy

Tropical Treats offers a unique, all-natural snack made with real coconut, pineapple, and ginger, available in three sizes (small $150, medium $250, large $350). The product appeals to students looking for a healthy energy boost during study sessions or social activities.

Price Strategy

Our pricing is structured for student affordability while maintaining profitability:

  • Small Pack: $150 - Entry-level sampling
  • Medium Pack: $250 - Best value for regular use
  • Large Pack: $350 - Bulk pricing for serious users

Place (Distribution Strategy)

Multi-channel distribution approach:

  • E-commerce Website: Primary sales channel
  • Social Media Shops: Instagram Shop integration
  • Campus Delivery: Student-run delivery system
  • Local Partnerships: Campus events and local stores

Promotion Strategy

Social Media Marketing

Instagram content, TikTok videos, student influencer partnerships, and viral marketing campaigns.

Campus Marketing

Free sampling, event sponsorships, student organization partnerships, and campus ambassador programs.

Digital Marketing

Email newsletters, WhatsApp marketing, content marketing, and SEO optimization.

Brand Storytelling

Founder story emphasis, authentic Caribbean branding, and student-focused messaging.
