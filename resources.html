Frequently Asked Questions

Are the ingredients really all-natural? +

Yes! We use simple, real ingredients: coconut, sugar, pineapple, and ginger. No artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors are added to our Pine & Ginger Coconut Drops.

Is the price in USD or JMD? +

All prices listed are in JMD (Jamaican Dollars). We offer three sizes: Small ($150 JMD), Medium ($250 JMD), and Large ($350 JMD).

Do you ship outside of Jamaica? +

Currently, we offer islandwide shipping via J Post or local Kingston pickup. We are exploring international shipping options for the future.

How long do the coconut drops stay fresh? +

Our coconut drops stay fresh for up to 3 weeks when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. For best quality, consume within 2 weeks of purchase.

Are there any other allergens I should be aware of? +

Our product contains coconut, which may be problematic for individuals with tree nut allergies. Our facility is coconut-free except for our product, but we recommend those with severe allergies consult with their healthcare provider.

Can I place bulk orders for events? +

Yes! We offer bulk ordering for events, study groups, and organizations. Please contact us at Tropicaltreatsja@gmail.com for special pricing and delivery arrangements.

What makes Tropical Treats different from other snacks? +

Tropical Treats is unique because it's created by a student, for students. We combine authentic Caribbean flavors with the specific needs of students who need natural energy. Our product tells a story of entrepreneurship, culture, and wellness.
